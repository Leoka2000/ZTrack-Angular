Introducing zTag smart grazing

Our mission

The grazed animals feel much better outdoors, develop more beautifully, and are healthier. It is in everyone's interest to sustain grazing as much as possible worldwide.

This is significant not only from the perspective of animal health but also in terms of landscape maintenance and preserving biodiversity.

Modern technologies should be utilized in grazing as well. Using GPS satellites, we can track the movement of animals, and from sensor data, we can check their activity, health status, nutrition, or even their reproductive period. We are developing sophisticated sensors that animals can comfortably wear in their ears, allowing us to continuously monitor and ensure the safety of our livestock.
